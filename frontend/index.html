Features

Everything you need to automate your energy

From live rule execution to quick controls, Tesla EV charging, and audit history, SoCrates keeps the operating layer in one place.

Automation Engine

Rules evaluate every 60 seconds in the cloud. Combine price signals, battery SoC, time of day, solar and weather forecasts. First matching rule fires, with a full decision log.

Price & Weather

Blend live import and feed-in prices with forward price outlooks and multi-day weather forecasts. Forecast trends feed your rules so you can act before spikes or curtailment windows.

Automation Lab

Test any rule before going live. Simulate spikes, low SoC, or cloudy days and see exactly which rule fires and why, without touching your inverter.

Solar Curtailment Monitor

Automatically limits export when feed-in prices drop below your threshold. Set your floor price once and SoCrates enforces it every minute.

ROI & Automation History

See the dollar impact of every fired rule with daily, weekly, and monthly history. Track savings clearly and export your data anytime.

Quick Controls

Need to react fast? Trigger one-tap charge/discharge with power and duration controls, then return to automation with timed auto-resume.

Tesla EV Integration

Connect Tesla in Settings, monitor EV status in the dashboard, and send start, stop, and charge-limit commands when readiness checks pass.

Community Rules Library

Filter by category, difficulty, and scenario. Search instantly, import templates in one click, and customize with plain-English summaries.

Private & Secure

Per-user isolated accounts on Google Firestore. Inverter API keys are never returned to the browser after setup and never shared with third parties.

FAQ

Common questions

Can't find an answer? Send us a message.

Which inverter brands are supported?
FoxESS is fully supported today. AlphaESS, Sigenergy, and SunGrow integration is in development and coming soon. More brands are on the roadmap — let us know which you'd like to see next.
Do I need to be an electrician or developer?
No. SoCrates connects to your inverter through the manufacturer's existing cloud API using your device serial number and API key. No hardware modifications, no coding — just copy your credentials from the inverter app and paste them into the setup wizard.
Which electricity retailers / price feeds are supported?
Currently Amber Electric is supported for live and forecast prices in Australia. Support for additional retailers and markets is planned. Static time-of-use schedules work without any price feed.
Do you support EV integration?
Yes. Tesla EV integration is available now through Settings. You can connect by VIN with OAuth, view EV readiness and status on the dashboard, and use start/stop/set-charge-limit controls when Tesla command prerequisites are met.
How does automation work — is it always-on?
Yes. SoCrates runs a cloud scheduler every minute that evaluates your rules in priority order. The first rule whose conditions are all met sends a command to your inverter. You can pause automation globally with the Master toggle any time.
Is my inverter API key stored securely?
Credentials are stored encrypted in Google Firestore with per-user access rules. They are never returned to the browser after initial setup or shared with third parties.
Will the first 500 users stay free?
Yes. Early members get free access. If and when pricing changes for new sign-ups in the future, we're committed to giving early members ample notice and fair options. Right now, the focus is on building something genuinely useful.
What happens after the 500 spots fill up?
Pricing for sign-ups beyond the first 500 is TBD — community-first, nothing extractive. We'll involve early members in that conversation before anything changes. Right now, the focus is on building something genuinely useful.
