Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 March 2026

This page explains, at a high level, how SoCrates handles account information, connected-device configuration, and usage data when you use the platform or its public tools.

Information we collect

  • Account details such as email address and authentication identifiers.
  • Configuration data needed to connect supported devices and services.
  • Platform usage, diagnostics, and operational logs used to run, secure, and improve the service.
  • Input values you enter into public tools, where needed to calculate results in your browser or support product analytics.

How we use information

  • To authenticate you and provide access to your account.
  • To run automation, display device status, and support connected service integrations.
  • To troubleshoot issues, monitor service health, and improve reliability and product quality.
  • To protect the platform, users, and infrastructure from abuse or unauthorized access.

Storage and security

Configuration and operational data is stored using Google Firebase and related infrastructure. Sensitive credentials are intended to be protected with access controls and service-side handling. No internet-connected system can be guaranteed perfectly secure, but we aim to minimize exposure and limit access appropriately.

Third-party processors and integrations

SoCrates may rely on third-party services including Firebase, supported inverter vendors, Tesla, weather sources, and electricity pricing providers. Data exchanged with those services is limited to what is needed to provide the relevant feature.

Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to operate the service, support the account, meet security and operational needs, and satisfy legal obligations where applicable.

Your choices

You can stop using the service at any time. If you want account or privacy help, contact us and we can review what action is appropriate for the request and the system constraints involved.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to socrates.team.comms@gmail.com.
