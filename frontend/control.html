🔬 Solar Topology Diagnostics

🔍 For AC-Coupled Solar Users: If your solar panels connect to a separate inverter rather than directly to the battery inverter, your setup is "AC-coupled". Some providers expose that production in a meter or generation channel instead of a PV field. Use these tools to identify which telemetry source represents your solar production.

📖 How to Use These Diagnostics

Step 1: Click "Discover Available Variables" to see what data your device exposes.

Step 2: During solar production hours (midday), click "Get All Real-Time Data".

Step 3: Check the "API Response" panel above for:

  • pvPower - DC-coupled solar (normal setup)
  • meterPower and meterPower2 - Meter channels (may be AC-coupled solar)
  • batChargePower - Battery charging rate
  • gridConsumptionPower - Grid import

Analysis: If pvPower ≈ 0 but battery is charging and no grid import, energy comes from AC-coupled solar (check which meterPower* has positive value).
